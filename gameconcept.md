# Mars Inc.

This is a single player based strategy game where the player takes on the role of a CEO of a company aiming to colonize Mars. The player must manage resources, build infrastructure, and navigate challenges to establish a successful colony on the Red Planet. 

The game data is stored in local storage, the game is like an idle miner tycoon game but with a mars colonization theme and more complex management mechanics. 

The player starts with a small budget and basic equipment as well as a Company, and must make strategic decisions to grow their company and expand their operations on Mars. The game features various challenges such as resource scarcity, environmental hazards. The player must level their company, rockets, and colonies to unlock new technologies and capabilities.
